Search the Library

The main University of Alberta Library search box gives you access to full-text articles, books, e-books, journals, databases, articles, media and more. When you begin your search you will be able to choose the following categories:

  • Everything: Library Catalogue and Articles.
  • Library Catalogue: Online and physical resources in the University of Alberta Library collection.
  • Articles: Peer-reviewed journal articles, magazine articles, book chapters, and more from the Library collection and Open Access sources.

More Search Options

Search Beyond the Library: On the search results page you can expand your search to include a global index of over 5 billion scholarly publications. Beyond the Library results will include items that the Library does not have immediate access to. These can usually be requested through Interlibrary Loan. Course Materials: Search for course materials by course code, title or instructor name using Talis Aspire.
