Search the Library

The main University of Alberta Library search box gives you access to full-text articles, books, e-books, journals, databases, articles, media and more. When you begin your search you will be able to choose the following categories:

  • Everything: Library Catalogue and Articles.
  • Library Catalogue: Online and physical resources in the University of Alberta Library collection.
  • Articles: Peer-reviewed journal articles, magazine articles, book chapters, and more from the Library collection and Open Access sources.

More Search Options

  • Search Beyond the Library: On the search results page you can expand your search to include a global index of over 5 billion scholarly publications. Beyond the Library results will include items that the Library does not have immediate access to. These can usually be requested through Interlibrary Loan.
  • Course Materials: Search for course materials by course code, title or instructor name using Talis Aspire.

Statement on Harmful Language in
Catalogue Records and Resource Descriptions

The University of Alberta Library recognizes that our catalogue and resource descriptions contain language that reflects the biases, norms, and perspectives of the time in which they were created. In particular, for resources about persons and groups, this language is often outdated and harmful.
