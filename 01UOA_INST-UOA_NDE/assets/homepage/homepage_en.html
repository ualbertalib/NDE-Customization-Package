Search the Library

What can you search?

The main University of Alberta Library search box gives you access to full-text articles, books, e-books, journals, databases, media and more. Choose from the following categories:

  • Everything: Library Catalogue and Articles
  • Library Catalogue: Online and physical resources in the UA Library collection
  • Articles: Peer-reviewed articles, book chapters, and Open Access sources

More search options

Search Beyond the Library

Expand your search on the results page to include a global index of over 5 billion scholarly publications. Items not immediately available can usually be requested through Interlibrary Loan.

How can I get help?

If you have questions or need help accessing library materials, ask library staff on our live chat (real humans). You can also visit our library locations, call, email, or book a consultation.
